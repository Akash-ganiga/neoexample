Professional Healthcare Solutions

NeoExample Medical & Healthcare is a Bengaluru-based startup led by experienced entrepreneurs in HR, Training, Healthcare, and Travel. We specialize in providing comprehensive healthcare staffing solutions and personalized homecare services.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between qualified healthcare professionals and those in need of medical assistance, whether in hospitals, care centers, or the comfort of home. We cater to individuals with chronic conditions, the elderly, and post-operative patients, enhancing their quality of life through personalized care.

With NeoExample, you get access to verified doctors, nursing staff, physiotherapists, caregivers, and attendants – all delivered to your doorstep with professionalism and compassion.
